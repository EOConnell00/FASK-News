FASK News

FASK News is a friendly fake news source created by a group of school kids from Ecole Sainte-Anne, Fredericton, NB. This website is a project of ours created to sharpen our journalism skills as well as have a bit of fun mocking the outrageously high amount of fake news articles spreading around the internet in this era. The majority of news articles found on FASK News are completely fake and all articles are not to be used as a credible source. Thank you for visiting our website and enjoy your reading.
